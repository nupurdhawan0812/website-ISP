About Us

What is IndiaSkillPedia Foundation?

IndiaSkillPedia Foundation [ISPF] is a society registered on 23 Apr 2015 with the Registrar of Societies Kamrup (Metropolitan) District, Assam under the Registration of Societies Act 1861. The Purpose of ISPF is to make available know-how on technologies, products, DIY methods and mentoring with training and hands on experiences to stablish enterprises, workshop infrastructure and enable entrepreneurs manufacture products, machines and harnessing technologies, manage their enterprises, sustain, grow their businesses and contribute to the communities at large.
